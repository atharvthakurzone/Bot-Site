Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We've got answers. Here are some of the most common queries we receive.

What is ZyncPay?

ZyncPay is a Telegram-based platform that allows users to earn a daily income by activating one of our plans. Users can also earn significant bonuses by referring new members to the platform.

Is this a safe investment?

We use secure payment gateways for plan activation and a transparent system for tracking earnings. However, like any platform involving financial returns, users should understand the model and participate responsibly. We prioritize platform stability and user security.

How do I withdraw my earnings?

Once you have at least one active referral, you can request a withdrawal directly from the bot's Wallet section. The minimum withdrawal amount is ‚Çπ250. Requests are processed by our admin team and sent to your provided UPI ID.

How long does it take for my account to be activated?

After you make a payment and upload the screenshot in the bot, our admin team will verify it. Activation is typically completed within a few hours, but please allow up to 24 hours.

How does the referral system work?

When a new user joins using your unique referral code and activates a plan, you receive a one-time bonus. The bonus amount is a percentage of their plan's cost, as detailed on our Plans page. Your referral also needs to be 'active' (i.e., have their own plan) for you to be eligible for withdrawals.

Can I increase my withdrawal limit?

Yes. Your withdrawal limit increases each time one of your referred users activates a plan. The higher the plan they choose (Basic, Plus, or Elite), the larger the increase to your personal withdrawal limit. This system rewards you for building a strong and active referral network.

What are Membership Levels?

Membership Levels (from Bronze to Legend) are ranks you achieve as your total withdrawal limit grows. Ranking up is a sign of your progress and success within the ZyncPay community. You can check your current level in the "Profile" section of the bot.
